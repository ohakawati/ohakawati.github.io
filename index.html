About the League

Our Mission

KxP is a nonprofit organization that channels the energy of competitive sports into meaningful community impact. Through our flag football league and other events, we raise funds and awareness for charities that matter.

The League

Our flag football league brings together athletes of all skill levels for a fun, competitive season. Teams compete weekly with full stats tracking, standings, and playoff contention.

Get Involved

Whether you want to play, volunteer, or sponsor a team, there's a place for you in KxP. Reach out on our Instagram to learn how you can be part of the next season.
